My friend has been a victim of sexual assault, how can I help?

  • Listen to your friend
  • Believe them
  • Do not push them to answer your questions
  • Be non-judgemental
  • Do not force them to make certain decision
  • Encourage them to get help, refer them to Penn's resources.
  • Let them share as much or as little as they want to
  • Make sure you help yourself, too!
